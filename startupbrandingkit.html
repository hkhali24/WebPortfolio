Start Up Branding Kit Project

Creating a start-up branding kit for an interior design company was a unique experience. This branding package was designed for a fictional start up and includes:

  • Logo
  • Business Card Front and Back
  • Social Media Banners

In Adobe Photoshop, these materials were created in an artboard style which allowed me to see the colors, fonts, placement and sizing of the business content.
