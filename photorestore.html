Photo Restoration Project

In this project I explored restoring an old photograph using the tools available in Photoshop. This photograph was found on the WikiMedia Commons, Damaged Photographs, under the Faded color photographs category, titled Ujkigyos: Ujkigyos countryside. In this project I explored Adobe Photoshop Tools: Spot Healing Tool Brush & the Neural Filters. With the help of these powerful Adobe Photoshop tools restoring the damaged photograph was a success.

After cleaning up as much of the damage on the photograph as possible. Using the Spot Healing Tool Brush, I focused on the areas of the photo that had markings which were mainly around the horse and the sky. Then I applied Photoshop' Neural Filter, with the Colorize filter it added color to the faded photograph which brought the photo back to life.
